1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Improving site performance and functionality
  • Providing personalized content and features

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

Functionality Cookies

Remember choices you make to provide enhanced, personalized features.

Targeting Cookies

Used to deliver relevant content and advertising based on your interests.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser Settings: Configure your browser to accept, reject, or delete cookies
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy preferences on our website
  • Third-Party Tools: Use available tools to manage your cookie preferences

4. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Please check back regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our Cookie Policy, please contact us.
