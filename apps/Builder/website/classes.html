Explore Your Creativity

At Studio Nova, we offer a vast array of art classes designed to nurture your creative spirit. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artist, you'll find a class that's right for you. Our professional instructors guide you through each step in a motivating and supportive environment.

Our Classes

Painting

Explore your artistic skills through various painting techniques. These sessions cover everything from watercolors to oil painting.

Sculpture

Experience the art of shaping and carving with our sculpture classes. Learn to work with clay, stone, and other materials.

Digital Art

Unleash your imagination with our state-of-the-art digital tools. This course covers graphic design, digital painting, and more.

Photography

Capture the world around you with our photography classes. Learn about composition, lighting, and post-processing techniques.
