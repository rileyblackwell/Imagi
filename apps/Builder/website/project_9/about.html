Introduction to Lizards

Lizards are a widespread group of squamate reptiles, with over 6,000 species found all over the world. They are known for their diverse sizes, colors, and habitats.

Habitat and Behavior

Lizards inhabit a wide range of environments, from deserts to rainforests. Their behavior varies greatly among species, with some being active during the day while others are nocturnal.

Diet

The diet of lizards can vary significantly, with some species being herbivorous, while others are carnivorous or insectivorous. Understanding their diet is crucial for their care in captivity.
