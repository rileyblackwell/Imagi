About Us

Leading the way in luxury dock craftsmanship in Arkansas.

Our Experience

As the most seasoned dock builders in Arkansas, we pride ourselves on our unparalleled expertise in crafting both post docks and docks equipped with gazebos.

Our adept team sources high-quality timber, ranging from local pine to unique options like petrified wood from Lake Michigan, to meet diverse customer preferences. We understand that each body of water and each client's vision is unique, which is why we prioritize customization and attention to detail in every project we undertake.

Our Philosophy

We believe in a customer-centric approach, ensuring that our clients have a seamless and satisfying journey from the initial consultation to the final reveal of their dock. Our dedication to quality craftsmanship and aesthetically pleasing designs sets us apart as leaders in the luxury dock building industry. We aim to exceed expectations by combining innovative engineering practices with traditional woodworking artistry.
