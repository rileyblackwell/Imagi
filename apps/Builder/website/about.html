Our Mission

At Luminance Gallery, we believe in the transformative power of art. Our mission is to create a space where artistic excellence meets cultural dialogue, fostering an environment where both established and emerging artists can showcase their unique perspectives to a global audience.

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1998 in the heart of New York's vibrant art district, Luminance Gallery has grown from a modest exhibition space to one of the most respected contemporary art galleries in North America. Our journey reflects our unwavering commitment to artistic innovation and cultural exchange.
