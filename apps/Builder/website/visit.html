Accessibility

Luminance Gallery is committed to ensuring our space is accessible to all visitors. Our facility features:

  • Wheelchair accessible entrance and galleries
  • Elevator access to all floors
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Large print exhibition guides
  • Service animals welcome

For specific accessibility requests or questions, please contact us in advance of your visit.
