Care Tips

  • Ensure a proper habitat with enough space and the right temperature.
  • Provide fresh food and water regularly to maintain their health.
  • Handle your lizard gently to build trust and reduce stress.
  • Consult with a vet for regular health check-ups and advice on diet.
  • Research specific needs for each lizard species to ensure optimal care.
