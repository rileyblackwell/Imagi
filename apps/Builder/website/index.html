Interesting Lizard Facts

  • Lizards can detach their tails to escape predators.
  • Some species can change color to blend into their surroundings.
  • Lizards are generally cold-blooded and rely on external heat sources.
  • They can see in color and have a wide field of vision.
  • Many lizards can regrow their tails after losing them.
