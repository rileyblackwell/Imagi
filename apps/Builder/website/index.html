  1. Consultation to understand your pet's specific needs.
  2. Bathing with high-quality, pet-friendly products.
  3. Detailed grooming and styling tailored to your pet's breed.
  4. Final touches including nail trimming and cleaning ears.
  5. Feedback and recommendations for maintaining your pet's grooming at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I groom my dog?

It depends on the breed. Generally, dogs with longer hair require more frequent grooming, while short-haired breeds can be groomed less often.

Do I need an appointment?

We recommend making an appointment to ensure the best service and availability for your pet.
