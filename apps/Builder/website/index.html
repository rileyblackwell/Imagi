Welcome to Paws & Claws Grooming

Your Pet’s Best Friend

Why Choose Us?

At Paws & Claws, we provide top-notch grooming services to ensure your pet looks and feels their best. Our experienced staff treats your furry family members with the utmost care and respect.

We understand that grooming is not just about aesthetics; it's an essential part of your pet's health. Regular grooming helps prevent matting, reduces shedding, and enhances your pet's comfort and wellbeing.

Our grooming salon is equipped with the latest tools and products that are safe and effective, ensuring the best results for every pet.

Our Services

  • Full Grooming: From wash to clipping, we offer comprehensive grooming services tailored to your dog's breed and needs.
  • Bath & Brush: A refreshing bath and thorough brushing to keep your pet clean and shiny.
  • Nail Clipping: Essential nail trimming services to ensure your pet's paws are comfortable and healthy.
  • Specialty Cuts: Customized hairstyles and trims that suit your pet’s personality and lifestyle.

Each service is designed to meet your pet's individual needs, and we take the time to ensure your pet enjoys their grooming experience. Discover more about our services.
